Featured Case Study

Campus Ministry Office Website: A Comprehensive Approach

Challenge: The Campus Ministry Office needed a modern, accessible website to reach students and provide up-to-date information about events, services, and resources.

Solution: I developed a responsive website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that prioritizes user experience and information accessibility. The design incorporates the university's branding while maintaining a welcoming and spiritual atmosphere.

Results: The website successfully increased student engagement with ministry programs and streamlined communication between the office and the student body.

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Responsive Design, Accessibility Standards
